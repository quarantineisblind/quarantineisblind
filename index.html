Bored at home? Sick of aimless swiping? It’s time to find something real. Quarantine is Blind is a social-distance-friendly blind conversation experiment to help you form genuine romantic connections. Our unique matching process and fun conversation starters will help you find someone you honestly spark with so that you can write your own quarantine love story, even beyond stay-at-home orders.

The journey goes like this:

1. You tell us your type by liking photos of users.

2. Each week, we’ll release your top matches, but this time you won’t see their photos so you won’t know who they are.

3. During the week, you and your matches will chat and have blind audio dates. We will provide some fun conversation starters and games.

4. If you and your match mutually decide to continue, your pictures will be revealed!

This new way of dating will help you form deeper connections with matches while also adding a bit of excitement to the dating process. Don’t forget to share our app with friends and stay safe! ❤
